MY FIRST WEEK AT AKIRACHIX

My first 4 days at Akirachix were eventful and full of happiness.I was oveerjoyed on arrival to AkiraChix because now,
I have a chance to change my life and make my future literally. My parents and small brother accompanied me and were also
very optimistic. Although they had there doubts, this was cleared after they talked with Linda, who is the managing director. Food was
served to them and before that, we were welcomed with tea. It was such a warm welcome that will be commemorated in our minds for a while.

    GOOD THINGS I EXPERIENCED

  1. Ambience - The environment of AkiraChix is very greeny, beautiful and silent, making it condusive for learning.

  2. Warm welcome - The alumnis were very kind in guiding us and making sure we understood each and every step, moreso because we had came later than the other students.

  3. Good communication - The managing director helped our parents in telling them what the programme entailed.This helped in settling them

  4. Weather - The weather compared to my hometown was better because in Kissi, it was rainy but here it is sunny all the time
